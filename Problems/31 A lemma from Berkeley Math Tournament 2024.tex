The incircle of scalene triangle $\triangle{ABC}$ is tangent to $\overline{BC}, \overline{AC},$ and $\overline{AB}$ at points $D, E,$ and $F,$ respectively. The line $EF$ intersects line $BC$ at $P$ and line $AD$ at $Q.$ The circumcircle of $\triangle{AEF}$ intersects line $AP$ again at point $R \neq A.$
